Appel Community Website

Appel is a game made on Scratch created by Griffpatch. The game is a platformer where players control a character named Appel as he adventures through Appelmoshapje, collecting the golden apples and avoiding enemies and obstacles. The game has a dedicated community of speedrunners, level designers, and kaizo players who play through the game fast, create custom levels, and beat the hardest levels in the game.

Play the game here: https://scratch.mit.edu/projects/60917032/

This site was created to serve as a hub for the Appel community, hosting links to other community websites and important resources in the Appel community.
